Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers

What is THE JOB PROJECT?

The Job Project is a volunteer project for helping alumni and fresh graduates of IIT Guwahati to make the next career move. Currently we are focusing on members affected by Covid19 whose offers got rescinded/deferred and Alumni who got laid off.

How do you help me getting discovered?

Alumni's are the biggest resource for your career, but they often find it difficult to share individual profiles to their own network. We make it effortless for them to get the word out. Also the Opportunities section allows us to curate opportunities beyond the alumni network

Will this portal help me find Internships?

Currently the aim is to curate Job opportunities, Please do not fill the form for Internship related profiles. Although keep checking the resources section coming soon to stay updated with opportunities.

Do you reach out to Companies on our Behalf?

We do not reach out to companies individually. The Job Project is a curation of talent from IITG and related opportunities. We focus on discovery aspect on both sides, your campus cell works on the match-making part.

Are you officially associated with the Institute?

We are a volunteer group comprising of students and alumni of IITG, In no way this project is officially connected with IIT Guwahati or the Placement cell of IIT Guwahati.

Who should we contact for nuanced queries?

The website is maintained by Aniket Mandle and Sai Bhaskar Devatha. You can reach out to us at any of out social handles or at aniketmandle@iitg.ac.in
